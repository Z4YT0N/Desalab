Our Technology

The Water Desalantion simulator combines advanced mathematical modeling with an intuitive user interface to provide accurate simulations of solar desalination processes. Our platform features:

  • Precise calculations of mass flux and efficiency
  • Support for multiple absorber geometries
  • Real-time data visualization
  • Comprehensive analysis tools
  • User-friendly interface
